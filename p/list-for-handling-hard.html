List For Handling Hard

Lately my work has been very challenging. No doubt, much of it is self-induced :) in the pursuit of BTTY. Whether at work or in life this is the list I TRY to use when I bump into something really hard, get overwhelmed, or frankly just want to quit. Hope you find it helpful.

  1. Focus on what you can control

  2. Stay positive

  3. Communicate, constantly

  4. Be consistent, but flexible

  5. Don't forget the mission

A Quote
