Download for Mac (167MB)

macOS only. Works on Apple Silicon and Intel.

After downloading

  1. Open the .dmg and drag "type" into Applications.
  2. Right-click (Control-click) the app, choose Open, then Open again. (A normal double-click shows a message saying Apple cannot check it for malware, with no Open button. Right-click is the way around it.)
  3. After the first open it launches normally from then on.

The app is unsigned, which is why macOS asks the first time. Source is on GitHub.
